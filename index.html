Welcome to the Texas Association of Tornado Watchers (TATW) site. We are a group of meteorologists and weather enthusiasts who are dedicated to tracking and documenting tornadoes in the state of Texas. Our goal is to provide accurate and up-to-date information on tornadoes in Texas, as well as to raise awareness about the dangers of these severe weather events. We hope that our website will be a valuable resource for anyone who is interested in learning more about tornadoes in Texas.
